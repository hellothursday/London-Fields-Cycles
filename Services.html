Our fully-equipped workshop is run by Shaun an experienced CyTech Level 3 bicycle mechanic. Shaun works with Mike and Mantas(our CyTech Level 2 mechanics) to provide an extensive repair service for all types of bicycle, from puncture repairs to a full service. The workshop team are always happy to answer questions and will advise which level of servicing will be best for your bike and budget. We keep a detailed service history and aim to get to know you and your bike and develop a service plan that will help you get the most out of your bike and spread the cost of keeping it in top condition.

We run a drop-in repair clinic at 8am-9am on weekday mornings. Aimed at small repairs to help you keep you on the road, we’ll aim to get your bike back to you the same day. For smaller jobs (typically 15-30 minutes) we will endeavour to fix your bike while you wait where possible. Please note that we are a busy workshop so we do work on a first come first served basis. For larger jobs and during busy periods we might book you in for later that day or suggest dropping by on another day.

We also take bookings for larger services, from a tune up to a full rebuild. These spaces fill up quickly so give us a call to book a slot or drop into the shop and we can give you an idea of cost and get you in the diary.


We offer 3 levels of servicing for your bike. Details of what they include are below.

Call 0208 5250077 or email us to book a service!

Tune up Service

No parts replaced but bike needs gear and brake adjustment, wheel truing and general setting up. Typical scenario - bike is lightly used, has been in storage, or needs assembling from a box.

Includes:

Wipe down frame/forks

Inspect for wear/damage

Quote for existing work

Advise for longer term

True wheels

Lubricate components

Adjust gears

Adjust brakes

Tighten bolts to spec

Pump up tyres

General Service

Bicycle is usable but several parts are either broken or worn out and need replacing. Typical scenario - bike is in regular use. Parts are not included in the service cost. Includes:

Wipe down frame/forks

Inspect for wear/damage

Quote for existing work

Advise for longer term

True wheels

Clean transmission

Lubricate components

Adjust headset and BB

Adjust gears

Adjust brakes

Tighten bolts to spec

Pump up tyres

Test ride

* Includes labour for fitting regular parts

Full Service

Bike is worn out and needs to be rebuilt, or parts are to be assembled to new frame. Typical scenario - bike in poor condition or to be built from scratch. Parts are not included in the service cost.

Includes:

Wipe down frame/forks

Inspect for wear/damage

Quote for existing work

Advise for longer term

True wheels

Clean transmission

Lubricate component

Service headset and BB

Service hubs

Adjust gears

Adjust brakes

Tighten bolts to spec

Pump up tyres

Test ride

* Includes labour for fitting regular parts ** Headset/BB facing, etc. for new builds not included
